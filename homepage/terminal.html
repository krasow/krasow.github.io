David Krasowska is a Ph.D. candidate at Northwestern University, advised by Dr. Peter Dinda. His research began at Clemson University collaborating with Argonne National Laboratory on lossy compression for HPC scientific applications. He received the DOE Computational Science Graduate Fellowship for his graduate studies. Currently he explores scheduling across distributed heterogeneous systems with Dr. Pat McCormick at Los Alamos National Laboratory.
