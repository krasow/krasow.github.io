David Krasowska is a Ph.D. candidate at Northwestern University, advised by Dr. Peter Dinda. His research journey began during his undergraduate studies at Clemson University where he collaborated with Argonne National Laboratory to explore lossy compression for optimizations in HPC scientific applications.
